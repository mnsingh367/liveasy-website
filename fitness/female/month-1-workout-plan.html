Month 1 Workout Plan

For a 60 kg beginner woman: Gently tone the body

Goals & Rules

Goal of Month 1:

  • Build consistency & improve posture
  • Increase daily movement & reduce stiffness
  • Gently tone the body & avoid burnout

Rules for the Month

  • Workout duration: 20–30 mins
  • Intensity: easy to moderate (Rest whenever needed)
  • Focus on form, not speed
  • Walking daily if possible
  • No exercise should feel painful

WEEK 1 — “Start Easy”

Goal: Get comfortable moving again.

Monday — Full Body + Posture

Warm-up (5 min):

Arm circles × 20, March in place × 1 min, Shoulder rolls × 15, Hip circles × 15, Cat-cow stretch × 10

ExerciseReps
Bodyweight Squats10
Wall Pushups8
Glute Bridges12
Bird Dogs8/side
Band Pull-Aparts (or towel pulls)12
Standing Knee Raises15

Do 2 rounds. Cooldown: Chest stretch, Hamstring stretch, Neck stretch

Tuesday — Walking + Mobility

  • Walk: 25–30 mins brisk walk
  • Mobility (10 min): Cat-cow, Child’s pose, Shoulder stretch, Hip opener, Seated forward fold

Wednesday — Arms + Core

Warm-up: 5 mins light movement

ExerciseReps
Dumbbell Shoulder Press (light)10
Bicep Curls12
Tricep Kickbacks12
Dead Bugs8/side
Plank15 sec
Standing Side Crunches12/side

Do 2 rounds.

Thursday — Rest Day

Optional: light stretching or casual walk

Friday — Lower Body + Waist

ExerciseReps
Squats10
Glute Bridges15
Step-Ups10/leg
Side Leg Raises12/side
Standing Knee-to-Elbow15
Heel Touches15

Do 2 rounds.

Weekend — Fun & Rest

Saturday (Fun Movement Day): Choose ONE (30-45 mins) - dance workout, yoga, badminton, cycling, long relaxed walk, beginner Pilates. The goal is enjoyment.

Sunday (Full Rest): Relax completely.

WEEK 2 — “Small Progress”

Goal: Slightly increase workload. Keep same schedule.

  • Add a few reps
  • Slightly faster walking pace
  • Improve exercise form

WEEK 3 — “Confidence Week”

Goal: Feel stronger and more capable.

Changes

  • Monday: Incline pushups on a table/counter instead of wall pushups. (2-3 rounds)
  • Wednesday: Slightly heavier dumbbells if available. Plank for 20-25 sec.
  • Friday: Add slow squats (3 sec down) & better posture focus. Optional band around knees for glute bridges.
  • Walking: 30–40 mins

WEEK 4 — “Challenge But Comfortable”

Goal: Finish the month feeling proud, not exhausted.

Monday — Full Body Circuit

Squats (15), Incline Pushups (10), Glute Bridges (18), Bird Dogs (10/side), Band Pull-Aparts (15), March in Place (1 min).
Do 3 rounds. Rest 60-90 sec between rounds.

Wednesday — Arms + Core

Shoulder Press (12), Bicep Curls (15), Tricep Kickbacks (15), Dead Bugs (10/side), Plank (30 sec), Standing Side Crunches (15).
2-3 rounds.

Friday — Lower Body + Waist

Squats (15), Step-Ups (12/leg), Glute Bridges (20), Side Leg Raises (15), Heel Touches (20), Standing Knee-to-Elbow (20).
2-3 rounds.
