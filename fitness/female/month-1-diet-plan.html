Month 1 Diet Plan

For a 60 kg beginner woman: Beginner Fat Loss & Toning

Main Nutrition Targets

  • Protein: ~70–85g/day
  • Water: 2–2.5L/day
  • Fiber: vegetables/fruits daily
  • No calorie counting initially.

Very Simple Month 1 Diet Plan

MORNING (Immediately After Waking)

Choose ONE (No need for complicated detox drinks):

  • Warm water
  • Jeera water
  • Plain water + lemon
  • Normal water

BREAKFAST OPTIONS (Rotate)

Choose ONE daily:

Option 1 (Excellent high-protein)

  • 2 moong dal chilla
  • Mint chutney
  • Curd

Option 2 (Very quick)

  • Oats with milk
  • Nuts + banana
  • Chia/flax seeds

Option 3

  • 2 idlis + sambar
  • Extra sambar for protein

Option 4

  • Besan chilla
  • Curd

Option 5 (Fastest option)

  • Peanut butter toast + milk
  • Fruit

MID-MORNING (Optional)

Choose ONE:

  • Fruit
  • Buttermilk
  • Coconut water
  • Handful of nuts
  • Roasted chana

LUNCH (Keep It Simple)

Best structure: 1 protein + 1 carb + vegetables

Example A

  • 2 rotis
  • Dal or rajma/chole
  • Sabzi
  • Curd

Example B

  • Rice
  • Sambar
  • Paneer/tofu
  • Salad

EVENING SNACK

This is where many diets fail. Instead of heavy fried snacks, choose:

  • Tea/coffee + roasted makhana
  • Fruit
  • Protein smoothie
  • Roasted chana
  • Peanuts
  • Yogurt bowl

DINNER (Light but Satisfying)

Option 1

Khichdi + curd

Option 2

Roti + sabzi + paneer

Option 3

Soup + toast + tofu/paneer

Option 4

Rice + dal + stir fry vegetables

Important Food Guidelines

EASY HIGH-PROTEIN VEGETARIAN OPTIONS

Very important for toning. Keep these frequently:

  • Paneer, Tofu
  • Greek yogurt/curd, Milk
  • Dal, Chickpeas, Rajma
  • Sprouts, Soy chunks, Peanuts

BEST LOW-EFFORT FOODS FOR HER

Weekly Prep Items (Prepare once for 2–3 days):

  • Boiled chickpeas
  • Chopped vegetables
  • Curd, paneer
  • Sprouts, boiled potatoes, cooked dal

SIMPLE PROTEIN SMOOTHIE

Very useful after workouts or busy evenings. Blend:

  • Milk + Banana + Peanut butter + Oats
  • Optional: protein powder later.

WHAT TO REDUCE (Not Completely Remove)

  • Sugary drinks
  • Daily bakery items
  • Excessive fried snacks
  • Late-night sweets
  • Overeating while watching TV

WHAT SHE CAN STILL ENJOY

Just smaller portions, not daily:

  • Dosa, Biryani occasionally
  • Pizza sometimes, Desserts occasionally

Lifestyle Rules

SIMPLE WEEKLY STRUCTURE

  • Monday–Friday: Mostly balanced meals.
  • Saturday: Flexible meal/snack allowed.
  • Sunday: Normal homemade comfort food. No guilt eating.

Important For Lean Waist

The biggest changes usually come from:

  • Consistent walking
  • Protein
  • Reduced snacking
  • Better sleep, lower stress
  • Posture training

* Not starvation diets.
