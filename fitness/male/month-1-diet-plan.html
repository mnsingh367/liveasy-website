Month 1 Muscle Gain Diet Plan

For you (64 kg, muscle gain beginner restart)

Main Nutrition Targets

  • Protein: ~100–120g/day
  • Water: 2.5–3L/day
  • Goal: Gradual lean muscle gain
  • No strict calorie counting initially

You will likely need bigger meal portions than your wife, and 1 extra protein-focused snack/shake.

Daily Diet Plan

MORNING (Immediately After Waking)

Choose ONE:

  • Warm water / Jeera water / Lemon water / Plain water
  • Optional: black coffee before workout if training in morning

BREAKFAST OPTIONS (Same Base Meals, Bigger Portions)

Option 1

  • 3 moong dal chilla + mint chutney + curd
  • Add: 1 glass milk

Option 2 (Very good muscle-gain breakfast)

  • Oats with milk + banana + nuts + chia/flax seeds
  • Add: peanut butter

Option 3

  • 3 idlis + extra sambar
  • Optional: boiled eggs if you eat eggs

Option 4 & 5

  • Option 4: 3 besan chilla + curd
  • Option 5: Peanut butter toast + milk + fruit + handful of nuts (Fastest busy-day option)

MID-MORNING SNACK

Best option for muscle gain: roasted chana + peanuts. Or choose ONE:

  • Banana + peanuts
  • Buttermilk + roasted chana
  • Fruit + nuts
  • Coconut water or sprouts bowl

LUNCH (Mostly Same as Her)

Structure: 1 protein + 1 carb + vegetables

Option 1

3 rotis + dal/rajma/chole + sabzi + curd

Option 2

Rice + sambar + paneer/tofu + salad

Option 3

Rice + dal + soy chunks/paneer + vegetables

PRE-WORKOUT (Optional)

If hungry before gym, keep it light. Choose ONE:

  • Banana
  • Coffee + banana
  • Peanut butter toast
  • Small oats bowl

POST-WORKOUT (VERY IMPORTANT)

Within 1 hour after gym (matters more for you than for her):

Protein Smoothie

Milk + banana + peanut butter + oats (Optional later: protein powder)

OR: Curd + banana + roasted chana

EVENING SNACK

  • Tea/coffee + makhana, roasted peanuts, yogurt bowl, roasted chana, fruit, or sprouts chaat
  • Avoid: daily fried snacks, large bakery snacks

DINNER (Simple & Higher Protein)

Options

  • Option 1: Khichdi + curd + paneer/tofu side
  • Option 2: Roti + sabzi + paneer
  • Option 3: Rice + dal + stir fry vegetables + tofu/paneer
  • Option 4: Soup + toast + paneer/tofu

BEFORE BED (Good for Muscle Recovery)

Optional but useful simple recovery habit. Choose ONE:

  • Milk / Turmeric milk / Curd / Handful of nuts

Important Food Guidelines

IMPORTANT HIGH-PROTEIN FOODS FOR YOU

Try to include 2–3 daily. Most important for muscle gain: soy chunks, paneer, milk, curd, dal consistency.

  • Paneer, tofu, soy chunks
  • Greek yogurt, milk, curd
  • Dal, rajma, chickpeas, sprouts
  • Peanut butter, nuts

VERY LOW-EFFORT WEEKLY PREP

Prepare for both of you together (Huge time saver):

  • Boiled chickpeas, Sprouts, Cooked dal
  • Chopped vegetables, Paneer, Curd
  • Boiled potatoes, Overnight oats

Lifestyle Rules

SIMPLE RULES FOR LEAN MUSCLE GAIN

  • Focus On: protein at every meal, progressive workouts, sleep, hydration, consistency
  • Reduce: skipping meals, excessive junk food, sugary drinks, late-night binge eating
  • You do NOT need: extreme bulking, dirty bulk, ultra-clean bodybuilding diet

WHAT YOU CAN STILL EAT

Consistency beats restriction. Just not daily and avoid huge portions:

  • Dosa, Biryani occasionally
  • Pizza occasionally, Sweets sometimes

SIMPLE WEEKLY STRUCTURE

  • Monday–Friday: Mostly structured eating.
  • Saturday: One flexible meal/snack.
  • Sunday: Homemade comfort food. No guilt eating.
