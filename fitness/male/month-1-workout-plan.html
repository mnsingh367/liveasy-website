Month 1 Muscle Gain Workout Plan

5-Day Split: Beginner-Intermediate Hypertrophy

Goals & Rules

Goals for Month 1

  • Primary: Muscle gain
  • Secondary: Improve posture/mobility slightly
  • Focus: Chest, shoulders, back width, arms, legs
  • Style: Beginner-intermediate hypertrophy
  • Split: 5 workout days + 2 recovery days (50–70 mins/session)

Important Rules

  • Weeks 1–2: Learn form again, Leave 2 reps "in reserve", Moderate weights only.
  • Weeks 3–4: Increase weight slightly, Add 1–2 reps where possible, Push closer to failure on final sets.
  • Rest Times: Compound lifts (90 sec), Isolation exercises (45–60 sec)

Warm-Up (Before Every Workout)

5–7 mins: Arm circles, Shoulder rotations, Bodyweight squats, Cat-cow stretches, Light treadmill/brisk walking. (Do 1 light warmup set before first 2 exercises)

Progression Plan & Cardio

Progression

  • Week 1: Get comfortable again.
  • Week 2: Increase reps slightly.
  • Week 3: Increase weights by 2.5–5 kg (compounds) / 1–2 kg (isolation).
  • Week 4: Push harder: last set close to failure, improve form quality.

Cardio

  • Keep cardio low during muscle gain.
  • 2–3 light walks weekly.
  • Avoid excessive HIIT initially.
